# The Story of Meesho and the Golden Egg

Meesho is an average Egyptian guy. Maybe a student, maybe a fresh graduate. Doesn’t really matter.

One day he was scrolling YouTube and found a video titled **“I made $200 in one day working from home!”**

Then another video appeared:  
**“The freelancing secret nobody tells you.”**

Then another one:  
**“Learn programming in 30 days and change your life.”**

Meesho subscribed.

Then along came **Dr. Ali Productivity**, a very respectable YouTuber. Speaks clearly, looks professional, has a clean desk setup, two monitors, a fancy microphone, and perfect lighting. He explains that freelancing is the **golden opportunity** and anyone who misses it is wasting their potential.

Then comes **TechBoss Former-FAANG-Millionaire** with a course for **$49**.

“Learn coding from zero and start making money online.”

And just in case that was not enough, every family gathering has that one uncle who says:

> “Why aren’t you freelancing yet? People are making thousands of dollars from their laptops!”

Meesho believed them. How couldn't he?

---

## What happened next

Meesho bought a programming course.

Finished half of a course called **“Earn your first dollar online RIGHT NOW!!!”**

Then he decided maybe programming just **is not his talent**.

So he bought a design course.

He made a few logos using **Canva**, uploaded them to **Fiverr**, waited a month…

No orders.

So he searched for another course explaining  
**“why you are not getting orders.”**

Meesho is still at the beginning.

At some point he even got academically scammed and joined a Ramadan course called  
**“Basbousa with Soy Sauce.”**

---

## The problem is not Meesho

Meesho is not lazy.

He is not untalented either.

The real problem is that nobody told him the **full story from the start**.

Dr. Ali Productivity and TechBoss Former-FAANG-Millionaire are not necessarily lying.

They are just selling **the dream**, not **the reality**.

Freelancing is not a chicken that lays golden eggs.

**The advantages are real:**

- You can work from anywhere  
- You choose your working hours  
- Your income is not limited by a fixed salary

**The downsides are real too:**

- No health insurance (see the law and insurance section)
- No guaranteed paycheck at the end of the month if you did not get clients
- Nobody will fix your problems if work gets delayed

---

## The right mindset

Freelancing is not a golden egg.

It is simply choosing to work for yourself.

Just like any project, it requires **time and effort**.

People who actually make money freelancing did not succeed because they found some **hidden secret nobody tells you**.

They succeeded because they:

- Specialized in one thing and became good at it  
- Learned how to talk to clients  
- Continued working even when results were slow  
- Learned from mistakes instead of buying endless courses
